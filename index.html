Show Summary

Invincible is an animated superhero series based on the comic book by Robert Kirkman. The show follows Mark Grayson. a seemingly ordinary high school student, who discovers that he has inherited superhuman abilities from his father, Omni-Man. As Mark begins his journey as the hero Invincible, he quickly learns that the world of superheroes is far more complex and dangerous than he ever imagined.

While his father, Omni-man, is considered one of the Earth's most powerful heroes, Mark's path is filled with moral dilemmas, epic battles, and shocking truths about his family's dark legacy. The show explores themes of power, responsibility, family, and the cost of being a hero, all while navigating Mark's transition from a teenager trying to live a normal life to a superhero facing impossible choices.


Creator and Studio Information

Invincible is based on the comic book series created by Robert Kirkman, who is best known for his work on The Walking Dead. Kirkman is a prolific writer and one of the most influential figures in the comic book industry. He co-created Invincible with artist Cory Walker and writer Ryan Ottley, who contributed significantly to the comic's distinctive art style and its dynamic storytelling.

The Invincible animated series is produced by Skybound Entertainment, a multimedia company founded by Robert Kirkman. Skybound is also known for its work on The Walking Dead and other successful projects, including Outcast and Manifest Destiny. The series is produced by Amazon Studios and was developed by Simon Racioppa, known for his work on various animated TV shows.

Fun Facts

  1. Robert Kirkman, the creator of Invincible is also the creator of The Walking Dead
  2. The Invincible comic series ran for 144 issues from 2003 to 2018
  3. Mark Grayson (Invincible) is voiced by Steven Yeun, who also played Glenn in The Walking Dead
  4. Omni-Man was inspired by classic superhero archetypes, especially Superman, but with a much darker twist.
  5. The show is known for its extreme violence and unexpected twists, making it one of the most brutal superhero stories.
